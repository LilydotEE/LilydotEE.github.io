About

Lili Zhao (赵丽丽) is a researcher at China Mobile Research Institute, China Mobile Limited. She obtained her Ph.D. degree from the School of Information and Communication Engineering (SICE), University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), where she was advised by Prof. Jianwen Chen. She was a joint Ph.D. student with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (NTU Singapore) under the supervision of Prof. Kai-Kuang Ma (Fellow of Singapore Academy of Engineering, IEEE Life Fellow). From April 2017 to March 2018, she was a visiting Ph.D. student at the University of Hong Kong (HKU), with Prof. Yik-Chung Wu. She was fortunate enough to work closely with Prof. Siwei Ma, Prof. Shanshe Wang, Dr. Falei Luo, Prof. Chuanmin Jia, Prof. Liaoyuan Zeng, Prof. Wenyi Wang, and Prof. Rumin Zhang.

Her research interests include video and point cloud processing, particularly video/point cloud compression/coding, video/point cloud frame interpolation. She also works on some standard works, including IEC/ISO MPEG, AVS, ITU-T, etc.

We are looking for research interns. Please feel free to contact me.
